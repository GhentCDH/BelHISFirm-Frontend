How the database is organised and what each view contains

BELHISFIRM is a selection of the SCOB database (Study Center for Companies and Exchanges, University of Antwerp), providing structured historical firm-level data derived from Belgian corporate records published in the Annexes of the Belgian Official Gazette (Le Moniteur Belge) since 1873. The dataset is designed to support research on long-term corporate dynamics and firm trajectories in Belgium.

The database is organised into two main views:

Corporations

This view contains structured information on Belgian companies, including incorporation and dissolution data, legal form, registered address, name changes, and other recorded corporate events.

Users can search and filter the data using the variables available on the website interface.

Information on individuals (e.g. shareholders and persons holding corporate functions) is not included due to GDPR constraints. These data are available within the broader SCOB research infrastructure at the University of Antwerp upon request.

Securities

This view contains a selected subset of securities data. It includes shares and bonds (obligations) listed on the Belgian stock exchanges, restricted to the last trading day of each year. This selection is intended to provide a longitudinal overview of financial instruments available on the market over time. It does not represent a complete reconstruction of all historical securities.

For access to more detailed securities data, please contact SCOB at the University of Antwerp.
