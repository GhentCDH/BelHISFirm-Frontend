About the data and the BelHisFirm project

About BelHisFirm

This website is part of the BelHisFirm research infrastructure. BelHisFirm provides long-term, firm-level data to support research in the social sciences. It complements available macroeconomic statistics with digital micro-level data extracted from printed sources and tools that help researchers explore these data. The data, in a nutshell, includes financial information on individual firms, as well as biographical information on their owners and managers.

BelHisFirm is funded by FWO Research Foundation Flanders and jointly developed by research groups from the University of Antwerp and Ghent University.

Data

The data in this portal represent a selected part of the larger SCOB Database. SCOB has been documenting Belgian stock exchange history since 1999. Its database contains hand-collected information on all securities listed on the Brussels and Antwerp stock exchanges. These include equity and debt issued by both companies and public institutions.

The information comes from:

  • Official stock exchange lists
  • Historical newspapers
  • Recueil financier (1893 to 1975)
  • Memento der Effecten (1945 to 2011)

These sources and their contents are discussed in detail in the online appendix to Annaert, J, Buelens, F. & De Ceuster, M. (2012). New Belgian stock market returns: 1832-1914. Explorations in Economic History, 49(2), pp. 189-204. doi:10.1016/j.eeh.2011.11.004

Belhisfirm enriches these data with newly digitised material from the Appendices to the Belgian Official Gazette (FR: Moniteur belge, NL: Belgisch Staatsblad, DE: Belgisches Staatsblatt). Since 1873, this publication has been the main source of official information on Belgian companies. It includes:

  • Articles of incorporation and partnership contracts, as well as modifications thereof (documenting corporate reorganisations such as mergers, name changes, and liquidations)
  • Company names, addresses, and legal forms
  • Appointments and dismissals of directors and auditors
  • Balance sheets and profit-and-loss accounts (until 1975)
  • Information on public securities issuance
  • Information on owners (shareholders and partners)

To comply with GDPR, personal data about individuals are not published online. Information about persons in company roles, such as directors or auditors, is available only through the SCOB Database after a formal request.

Data Reuse

This platform is a test version of the BelHisFirm infrastructure. The project is still under development, and the current portal does not represent the final version. You may use the available data at your own risk. The platform allows you to export the data that are currently accessible. In later stages of the project, additional tools and datasets will be archived through platforms such as Zenodo.
